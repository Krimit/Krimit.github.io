My Music

I love music. I majored in music composition and theory during my undergrad, which is where I picked up classical composition. I am also a guitarist with an affinity for rock/blues/metal. Here are some of my works, in a roughly chronological order.

Intermezzo

Composed for Prof. Yinam Leef's harmony class in 2010, this Intermezzo in the style of Brahms is a piece I am very fond of. Although not really much more than an exercise in 19th century form and harmony, I am very proud of the results. Performed by my lovely wife, Talia Amar.

Fear of Commitment

Written by Ben Draiman with 'Something for the Pain' in 2009. I arranged and recorded the song, and played all guitars.

Attrition

Ben Draiman with 'Something for the Pain', 2009. I arranged and recorded the song, and played all guitars. Guitar solo is missing.

Conversations

Ben Draiman with 'Something for the Pain', 2009. I arranged and recorded the song, and played all guitars.

Crickets

Crickets is an electronic piece, composed solely through the manipulation of recordings of crickets. Composed for Prof. Menachem Zur's Electronic Music course, 2009.

You Know, Son...

Violin+ Piano. One of my first compositions.

Cheeseburger Wars

Metal tune from 2003. I played guitars and bass.
